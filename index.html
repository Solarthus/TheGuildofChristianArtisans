Our Vision

“To see artisans and students formed in obedience to JESUS CHRIST, restoring the dignity of work, strengthening His Church, and bearing faithful witness through craftsmanship offered unto The LORD.”

Our Mission

“To disciple artisans and students through apprenticeship, mentorship, and service, forming skill and character together under the Lordship of JESUS CHRIST, in faithful partnership with His Church.”

The Guild Gospel Blueprints

How theology meets the workbench.

1

Design

GOD is the Master Craftsman. We were designed to create and steward what He has created.

2

Fracture

Sin corrupted the work. What was meant for worship became toil or pride.

3

Restoration

JESUS redeems the worker. As more than a carpenter's son, He shows us the meaning of labor.

4

Commission

We are sent to build faithfully. Our tools and talents become instruments of service.

Our Core Values

Christ Centered Faithfulness

Our work and our lives submit first to JESUS CHRIST and His Word. Skill without obedience is hollow.

Integrity of Hand and Heart

We do what is right even when unseen, because God sees both the workbench and the heart.

Excellence as Worship

We pursue excellence because our work is offered unto The LORD. Precision is a reflection of whom we serve.

Servanthood Through Craft

We use our gifts to serve others and build what blesses. Craftsmanship is a means of love and restoration.

Brotherhood & Apprenticeship

Iron sharpens iron. Masters teach willingly, students learn humbly, and no one stands alone.

Stewardship of Calling

We honor the materials God provides and the callings He entrusts, recognizing both are given by The LORD.

Guild Leadership

Larry McGraw

Founder

Larry McGraw is the founder of The Guild of Christian Artisans, a Christian nonprofit dedicated to discipling artisans and students through craftsmanship, mentorship, and service under the Lordship of JESUS CHRIST.

His vision for the Guild is rooted in the biblical model of apprenticeship, where skill is passed through relationship, character is formed alongside competence, and labor is offered unto The LORD.

He serves to guard the mission and shepherd the vision, ensuring that the Guild remains CHRIST-centered and church-connected. He believes that artisans who walk in obedience and skill can become quiet but powerful witnesses.

Andrew Gratton

Lead Artisan & Guild Treasurer

Andrew Gratton is a master artisan with over twenty years of experience in traditional blacksmithing and wood carving. His life’s work reflects a deep commitment to craftsmanship, patience, and the faithful stewardship of skill, formed through years of hands-on practice and disciplined labor.

With a deep conviction that work is worship, Andrew approaches craftsmanship not merely as a trade to be mastered, but as a calling to be stewarded faithfully before The LORD. His work reflects the biblical model of apprenticeship, where skill is refined through patience, character is shaped through responsibility, and craftsmanship becomes an offering unto GOD rather than a pursuit of profit alone.

Andrew is committed to passing on both technical skill and spiritual integrity, believing that artisans who labor in obedience to CHRIST can serve as quiet but powerful witnesses in their families, churches, and communities. Through his mentorship and hands-on instruction, he seeks to cultivate excellence, humility, and faithfulness in the next generation of craftsmen.
